## Shared Patterns

1. High-risk patients tend to show both medication adherence issues and delayed follow-up.
2. Diabetes, hypertension, and chronic kidney disease cases need repeated monitoring and outreach.
3. Delayed follow-up is a stronger warning sign when it appears together with missed doses or irregular self-monitoring.
4. Even lower-risk patients can still need education when inhaler or daily medication use is inconsistent.

## Operational Notes

1. Prioritize outreach for patients whose follow-up is already delayed.
2. Reinforce medication counseling for patients with repeated missed doses.
3. Use both this narrative summary and the structured DB rows together during retrieval.
